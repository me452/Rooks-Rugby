The Mindset

Straight-Line Power

Rooks don't dance around. We play direct, physical, low-penalty rugby down the channels. We smash through, clear out, and reset the line.

The Fortress

Our defensive line is an unyielding wall. Built on discipline, fitness, and trust in the man next to you. Welcome to the frontline.

Brotherhood & Pride

Representing Greenville and East Texas. Whether you are a seasoned veteran or holding a rugby ball for the first time, there is a place for you on the pitch.
